MODERN CLASSIC RETURN

A return to the roots with modern innovation.

Back to Stealth and Storytelling

The Modern Classic Return represents the franchise's evolution as it honors its stealth-focused origins while embracing modern gameplay innovations. These titles mark a new chapter that balances classic Assassin's Creed gameplay with contemporary design.

Experience Basim's transformation from street thief to Master Assassin in the Golden Age of Baghdad, then journey to Feudal Japan with dual protagonists Yasuke and Naoe, combining brute force with deadly precision in one of gaming's most requested settings.

These games showcase refined parkour, focused narratives, and a return to the social stealth and assassination gameplay that defined the early franchise, while incorporating lessons learned from the RPG trilogy.
