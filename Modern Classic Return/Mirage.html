Legacy & Impact

Assassin's Creed Mirage marks a return to the series' roots, focusing on stealth, parkour, and a tightly crafted narrative. Set in the vibrant city of Baghdad during the Islamic Golden Age, it pays homage to the original game while introducing modern refinements. Basim's story bridges past and future, connecting to the larger AC universe.
