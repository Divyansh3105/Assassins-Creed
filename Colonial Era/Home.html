THE KENWAY SAGA

Three generations. One legendary bloodline.

The Colonial Era

The Kenway Saga spans three generations of one of the most legendary families in Assassin's Creed history. From the Golden Age of Piracy to the American Revolution, witness the rise and fall of the Kenway dynasty.

Experience Edward Kenway's transformation from ruthless pirate to Master Assassin in the Caribbean, follow Haytham Kenway's path as a Templar Grand Master, and join Connor Kenway (Ratonhnhaké:ton) in his fight for freedom during the birth of a nation.

This saga explores themes of freedom, legacy, and the blurred lines between Assassins and Templars, featuring naval combat, exploration, and some of the franchise's most memorable characters.
