Pirate's Creed

Set sail as Edward Kenway, a reckless pirate captain with the skills of an Assassin. Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag blends naval combat, exploration, and classic stealth action in a lush open-world Caribbean. Become a legend of the seas during the Golden Age of Piracy.

Story Highlights

  • Experience Edward's transformation from pirate to Assassin
  • Explore the vast Caribbean with over 50 unique locations
  • Meet legendary pirates like Blackbeard and Anne Bonny
  • Command the Jackdaw in epic naval battles

Gameplay Mechanics

Naval Warfare

Command the Jackdaw in intense ship battles. Upgrade your vessel with mortars, swivel guns, and heavy shot. Board enemy ships and claim their cargo in the ultimate pirate experience.

Open World Exploration

Discover hidden coves, underwater shipwrecks, and Mayan ruins. Hunt whales and sharks, dive for treasure, and build your pirate fleet across the Caribbean.

Legacy & Impact

Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag is widely considered one of the best entries in the franchise. Its revolutionary naval combat system, vast open-world Caribbean, and compelling pirate narrative captivated players worldwide. Edward Kenway's journey from selfish pirate to noble Assassin resonated deeply, while the game's sea shanties became iconic. Black Flag proved that the series could successfully explore new time periods and gameplay styles.
