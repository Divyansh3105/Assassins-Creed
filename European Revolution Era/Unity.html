Release Date

Platforms

Setting

Era

Revolution in Paris

Arno Dorian seeks redemption during the French Revolution in a city boiling with chaos. Assassin's Creed: Unity introduced cooperative gameplay, breathtaking parkour mechanics, and stunningly recreated Parisian landmarks. From Notre-Dame to the Bastille, join a tale of betrayal, justice, and revolution.

  • Explore a meticulously recreated 18th-century Paris
  • Uncover the conspiracy behind Arno's father's death

Legacy & Impact

Assassin's Creed Unity pushed the boundaries of the franchise with its stunning recreation of Revolutionary Paris and innovative cooperative gameplay. Despite a rocky launch, the game's attention to historical detail, improved parkour system, and deep customization options earned it a dedicated fanbase. The game's portrayal of Notre-Dame became especially poignant after the 2019 fire.
