EUROPEAN REVOLUTION ERA

From the streets of Paris to the heart of London.

Revolution in Europe

The European Revolution Era takes players through two of history's most transformative periods: the French Revolution and the Industrial Revolution. These games showcase the franchise's evolution with enhanced parkour, cooperative gameplay, and dual protagonists.

In Unity, experience the chaos and beauty of Revolutionary Paris through Arno Dorian's quest for redemption. Then in Syndicate, command the Frye twins as they liberate Victorian London from Templar oppression during the height of the Industrial Age.

These titles introduced refined combat systems, massive crowds, detailed urban environments, and innovative gameplay mechanics that pushed the series forward while honoring its roots.
