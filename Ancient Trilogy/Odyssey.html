Forge Your Legacy

In Assassin's Creed: Odyssey, embark on an epic journey through ancient Greece during the Peloponnesian War. Choose your path as either Alexios or Kassandra, uncover your Spartan heritage, and shape history through your choices in this RPG-style adventure.

Story Highlights

  • Choose between Alexios or Kassandra as your protagonist
  • Make choices that impact the story and world
  • Explore ancient Greece from Athens to Sparta
  • Uncover the secrets of the First Civilization

Legacy & Impact

Assassin's Creed Odyssey expanded on Origins' RPG foundation with deeper choice-driven gameplay and branching narratives. Set in the stunning world of Ancient Greece, it offered unprecedented player agency and one of the largest maps in the series. The game's mythological elements and engaging characters made it a fan favorite in the Ancient Trilogy.
