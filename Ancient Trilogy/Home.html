ANCIENT TRILOGY

Witness the birth of legends in ancient worlds.

Exploring Ancient Civilizations

The Ancient Trilogy revolutionized the franchise with massive RPG elements, breathtaking open worlds, and stories set in humanity's most fascinating ancient civilizations. These games explore the origins of the Hidden Ones and the eternal conflict.

Journey through Ancient Egypt with Bayek, the founder of the Hidden Ones, explore the myths and legends of Ancient Greece with Kassandra or Alexios, and raid across Dark Ages England as Eivor, a legendary Viking warrior.

Featuring enhanced combat systems, dialogue choices, massive worlds to explore, and deep RPG mechanics, the Ancient Trilogy redefined what an Assassin's Creed game could be.
