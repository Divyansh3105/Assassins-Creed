Birth of the Brotherhood

Assassin's Creed: Origins takes players to Ancient Egypt, where the journey of Bayek of Siwa gives rise to the Assassin Brotherhood. Discover a rich open world, RPG elements, and a new combat system that reshaped the franchise.

Story Highlights

  • Witness the birth of the Assassin Brotherhood
  • Explore the vast deserts and cities of Ancient Egypt
  • Experience Bayek and Aya's quest for vengeance
  • Encounter Cleopatra and Julius Caesar

Gameplay Mechanics

RPG Combat System

Experience a completely revamped combat system with hitbox-based attacks, dodging, and parrying. Level up, customize your gear, and unlock powerful abilities in this action-RPG transformation.

Senu the Eagle

Use your eagle companion to scout locations, mark enemies, and discover secrets from above. Senu revolutionized reconnaissance in the franchise.

Game Gallery

Legacy & Impact

Assassin's Creed Origins marked a bold new direction for the franchise, introducing RPG mechanics, a revamped combat system, and a massive open world. Set in stunning Ancient Egypt, the game told the origin story of the Brotherhood while modernizing gameplay for a new era. Its success paved the way for Odyssey and Valhalla, establishing the "Ancient Trilogy" formula.
