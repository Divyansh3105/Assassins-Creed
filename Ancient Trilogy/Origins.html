Assassin's Creed: Origins (2017)

Birth of the Brotherhood

Assassin's Creed: Origins takes players to Ancient Egypt, where the journey of Bayek of Siwa gives rise to the Assassin Brotherhood. Discover a rich open world, RPG elements, and a new combat system that reshaped the franchise.

Revolutionized Combat

Engage in action-RPG combat with customizable gear and abilities.

Massive Open World

Explore deserts, cities, pyramids, and the Nile in stunning detail.

Senu the Eagle

Use your eagle companion Senu to scout, tag enemies, and explore from above.
