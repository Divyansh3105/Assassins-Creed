Become a Viking Legend

Assassin's Creed: Valhalla casts you as Eivor, a fierce Viking raider on a quest for glory in 9th-century England. Lead your clan from icy Norway to a new home in lush lands, engage in brutal battles, and forge alliances to establish your kingdom.

Story Highlights

  • Lead your Viking clan from Norway to England
  • Build and upgrade your settlement, Ravensthorpe
  • Forge alliances with Saxon kingdoms
  • Uncover the mysteries of the Hidden Ones and Order of Ancients

Legacy & Impact

Assassin's Creed Valhalla brought the Ancient Trilogy to a climactic conclusion with its Viking setting and settlement-building mechanics. The game's massive world, spanning England and Norway, combined with its deep narrative connections to the series' mythology, made it one of the most ambitious entries. Valhalla's post-launch support with expansions set new standards for the franchise.
