Revolution Begins

Assassin's Creed III introduces Connor Kenway, a half-Mohawk assassin fighting for freedom during the American Revolution. Navigate battlefields, scale forests, and influence a nation's birth. With a revamped engine and combat, AC3 redefined the series with epic scope and emotional storytelling.

Story Highlights

  • Experience Connor's journey from childhood to Master Assassin
  • Participate in iconic battles like Bunker Hill and Lexington
  • Navigate the complex relationship between Connor and Haytham
  • Witness the conclusion of Desmond's modern-day story

Gameplay Mechanics

Frontier & Hunting

Explore the vast American wilderness. Hunt animals for resources, discover hidden locations, and experience dynamic weather and seasons that affect gameplay.

Revamped Combat

Experience fluid, brutal combat with dual-wielding weapons, environmental kills, and the iconic tomahawk. Chain kills and counter attacks feel more visceral than ever.

Legacy & Impact

Assassin's Creed III concluded Desmond's story while introducing a bold new setting and protagonist. The game's ambitious scope, featuring massive battles, naval combat, and a sprawling frontier, pushed the series forward. Connor's journey through the American Revolution offered a unique perspective on history, and the naval gameplay laid the groundwork for Black Flag's success.
