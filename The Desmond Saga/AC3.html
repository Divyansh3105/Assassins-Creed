Assassin's Creed III (2012)

Revolution Begins

Assassin's Creed III introduces Connor Kenway, a half-Mohawk assassin fighting for freedom during the American Revolution. Navigate battlefields, scale forests, and influence a nation's birth. With a revamped engine and combat, AC3 redefined the series with epic scope and emotional storytelling.
