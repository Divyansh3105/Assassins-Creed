Assassin's Creed: Revelations (2011)

The Final Chapter of Ezio

Assassin's Creed: Revelations closes the legendary tale of Ezio Auditore. Journey to Constantinople to uncover the secrets of Altaïr’s legacy. New tools like the Hookblade, bomb crafting, and tower defense gameplay bring fresh dynamics. Experience both Ezio’s and Altaïr’s intertwined destinies in a moving finale.

Core Features

Hookblade

Speed through Constantinople and scale buildings faster with the new assassin tool.

Bomb Crafting

Create tactical bombs from over 300 combinations to suit any situation.

Legacy of Altaïr

Live through the memories of Altaïr and uncover the secrets of the ancient Brotherhood.
