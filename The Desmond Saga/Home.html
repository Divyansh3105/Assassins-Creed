The Desmond Miles Era

The Desmond Saga represents the foundation of the Assassin's Creed franchise, spanning from 2007 to 2012. This epic storyline follows Desmond Miles, a modern-day bartender who discovers his ancestral connection to legendary Assassins throughout history.

Through the revolutionary Animus technology, players experience the memories of Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad during the Third Crusade, Ezio Auditore da Firenze through the Italian Renaissance, and Connor Kenway during the American Revolution. Each ancestor's story builds toward Desmond's ultimate destiny in preventing a global catastrophe.

This saga introduced groundbreaking parkour mechanics, social stealth gameplay, and a rich narrative that blends historical fiction with science fiction, setting the standard for the entire franchise.
