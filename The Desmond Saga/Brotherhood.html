Assassin's Creed: Brotherhood (2010)

Release Date

November 16, 2010

Platforms

PC, Xbox 360, PS3, Mac

Setting

Rome, Italy

Era

1499-1507 AD

Brotherhood is Power

Continuing Ezio’s journey, Assassin's Creed: Brotherhood expands the story to Rome where Ezio must rebuild the Assassin Brotherhood. The game introduced the iconic Brotherhood system, allowing players to recruit and train fellow assassins. It also marked the franchise’s first foray into online multiplayer.

Core Features

Brotherhood System

Recruit, train, and call on fellow assassins to aid you in battle.

Rome Reborn

Help liberate and restore the city by investing in shops, landmarks, and guilds.

Multiplayer Debut

Engage in tense cat-and-mouse multiplayer with unique characters and playstyles.

Notable Characters

Ezio Auditore
Cesare Borgia
Niccolò Machiavelli

Gameplay Mechanics

Assassin Recruitment

Save citizens from guards and recruit them into your Brotherhood. Train them through missions and call upon them in combat for devastating chain kills.

Borgia Towers

Liberate districts by assassinating Borgia captains and burning their towers. Restore Rome's economy and unlock new shops and services.

Game Gallery

Legacy & Impact

Brotherhood proved that direct sequels could expand and improve upon their predecessors. The Brotherhood recruitment system became a franchise staple, and the multiplayer mode introduced innovative cat-and-mouse gameplay that stood out in the action genre. Rome's single massive city showcased the series' attention to historical detail.
