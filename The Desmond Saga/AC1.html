Assassin's Creed (2007)

The Birth of the Creed

Step into the robes of Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad, a master assassin during the Third Crusade. The first title in the Assassin's Creed series laid the foundation for stealth, parkour, and historical fiction in modern gaming. With an innovative open world, unique free-running mechanics, and the introduction of Desmond Miles, this game began a legendary journey.

Core Features

Free-Running Parkour

Climb rooftops and leap across ancient cities fluidly.

Social Stealth

Blend with crowds and vanish in plain sight.

Historical Narrative

A thrilling plot that fuses real history with sci-fi intrigue.
