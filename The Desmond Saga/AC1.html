The Birth of the Creed

Step into the robes of Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad, a master assassin during the Third Crusade. The first title in the Assassin's Creed series laid the foundation for stealth, parkour, and historical fiction in modern gaming. With an innovative open world, unique free-running mechanics, and the introduction of Desmond Miles, this game began a legendary journey.

Story Highlights

  • Explore three major cities: Jerusalem, Acre, and Damascus
  • Uncover the conspiracy behind the Templar Order
  • Experience the dual narrative of Altaïr and Desmond Miles
  • Discover the ancient conflict between Assassins and Templars

Gameplay Mechanics

Assassination Techniques

Master the art of silent kills with the iconic hidden blade. Plan your approach, blend with scholars, and strike from the shadows to eliminate your targets without detection.

Investigation & Intel

Gather information through eavesdropping, pickpocketing, and interrogation. Each assassination requires careful planning and reconnaissance before the strike.

Legacy & Impact

Assassin's Creed revolutionized open-world gaming with its innovative parkour system and historical storytelling. The game introduced the Animus concept, blending modern-day and historical narratives in a way never seen before. Its success spawned one of gaming's most beloved franchises, influencing countless action-adventure titles.
