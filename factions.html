Core Ideology

Tenets & Beliefs

Tenet I — Brotherhood

Stay Your Blade

Never harm an innocent. The Brotherhood serves to protect those who cannot protect themselves.

Tenet II — Brotherhood

Be Discreet

Never compromise the Brotherhood. Work in the shadows, revealing yourself only when necessary.

Tenet III — Brotherhood

Never Compromise

Always place the Brotherhood above personal gain. The creed survives only through unity.

Philosophy — Brotherhood

Free Will

Humanity must choose its own path. Freedom — even to make mistakes — is the highest ideal worth fighting for.

Tenet I — Order

Peace Through Control

True peace requires order. Humanity must be guided — not given the freedom to destroy itself.

Tenet II — Order

Unity Above All

A single, unified vision is stronger than divided freedoms. The Order must act as one body, one will.

Tenet III — Order

The Greater Good

Individual sacrifice is acceptable if it ensures the survival and prosperity of the many. The ends justify the means.

Philosophy — Order

Order & Guidance

Humanity is flawed. Without the Order's influence, civilization will always collapse into chaos and self-destruction.
