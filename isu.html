Artifact Classification

The Pieces of Eden

Technological remnants of immense power, capable of bending reality and controlling minds.

Class: Mind Control

The Apple

Designed to create illusions and control human minds. The most common Piece of Eden found throughout history.

Class: Manipulation

The Staff

Capable of energy manipulation and amplifying the user's power. Often used by rulers to assert dominance over populations.

Class: Regeneration

The Shroud

A healing artifact capable of regenerating tissue and curing ailments, though it cannot resurrect the dead.

Class: Communication

Crystal Skull

Enables communication with beings across great distances and can transmit memories of the deceased Isu.

Class: Weaponized

Sword of Eden

A blade capable of projecting a concussive energy field and inspiring loyalty in those who behold it.

Class: Amplifier

Koh-i-Noor

A crystalline amplifier that magnifies any nearby Piece of Eden to catastrophic levels. Called "Mountain of Light" by humans.

Historical Record

The Isu Era

Key events in the collapse of the First Civilization.

~1,000,000 BCE

Rise of the First Civilization

The Isu build a global civilization far surpassing anything humanity would later achieve, developing the Pieces of Eden as tools of governance and control.

Unknown Date

Project Anthropos

The engineering of humanity by the Isu to serve as a docile workforce. Neurotransmitters were implanted to ensure obedience to the Pieces of Eden.

Pre-Catastrophe

War of Unification

A global conflict among Isu factions for control, further weakening their society ahead of humanity's rebellion.

Eve's Uprising

The Human Rebellion

Led by Eve, a human-Isu hybrid who stole an Apple of Eden, sparking the war for freedom that would define all of human history.

~75,000 BCE

The Great Catastrophe

A coronal mass ejection from the sun wiped out most Isu and humanity alike, ending the First Civilization's dominion over Earth forever.
